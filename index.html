For a flawless bridal makeup look, start with a well-prepped and hydrated face to ensure long-lasting wear. Use a primer to create a smooth base and choose a high-coverage, long-wear foundation that matches your skin tone perfectly. Opt for waterproof eye makeup to withstand tears and a soft, romantic eyeshadow palette with neutral or warm tones. A subtle highlighter on the high points of your face will add a radiant glow. Finish with a long-lasting matte or satin lipstick and set everything with a setting spray to keep your makeup fresh throughout the day.
